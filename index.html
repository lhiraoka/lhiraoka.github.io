Probing the language of living cells

I am broadly interested in studying and probing biochemical signaling pathways, elucidating molecular mechanisms and structure–function relationships to drive innovative therapeutic discoveries — especially for neurobiological problems. My research spans both the basic and translational realms, from biochemical metalloenzyme characterization to therapeutic nanomaterials, regenerative medicine, and lipid nanoparticle drug delivery.

Research Interests

Biochemical Signaling Pathways

Investigating molecular mechanisms that govern cellular communication, with a focus on structure–function relationships that underpin disease and therapeutic intervention.

Neurobiological Therapeutics

Applying chemical biology to unmet challenges in neuroscience — dissecting pathway dysregulation and identifying molecular targets for next-generation treatments.
