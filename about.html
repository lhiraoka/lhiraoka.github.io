About Me

I am an incoming PhD student at Tulane University in the Chemistry Department, finishing my undergraduate degree in Chemistry at the University of California, Berkeley. I am broadly interested in studying and probing biochemical signaling pathways, elucidating molecular mechanisms and structure–function relationships to drive innovative therapeutic discoveries — especially for neurobiological and neurochemical problems.

My research experiences span both the basic and translational realms, ranging from biochemical metalloenzyme characterization to therapeutic nanomaterials, including regenerative medicine and lipid nanoparticle drug delivery.

Outside of academics, I enjoy listening to and performing music, swimming, and running!
